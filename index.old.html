Brickshire Golf Leagues

We are a great group of guys and gals that love the game of golf. Each week a different game is played. These games can consist of straight score, partner best ball, 4 man scramble, and many more. You must have a handicap before joining the league. This makes it fair for all experience levels of golf. Each week a player will put $7 dollars into the pot with the potential to win a lot more. We average anywhere between 10 and 40 golfers each week. The league will occasionally play other course leagues, home and away. Towards the beginning of fall, we hold our annual Brickshire Cup! We are always welcome to new members. If interested please contact us.

When Do We Meet?

Tee times usually begin for the group around 8:00 am. They are usually in shotgun fashion to keep the pace of play. Most members arrive around 7:00 am at Brickshire Golf Course to hit the range and prepare for that day's round.
