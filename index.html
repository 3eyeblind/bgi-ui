Brickshire Mens Golf League

About Us

We are a fun community of golf enthusiasts, welcoming both men and women who share a passion for the game. Our league hosts weekly games, ranging from straight score to partner best ball and more, ensuring a diverse and exciting experience for players of all skill levels. To maintain fairness, all members must have a handicap through GHIN. It is not required however when you first join. We will help you build a handicap playing with us. Each week, players contribute $7 to the pot, with the chance to win significantly more. Membership entails a $20 annual association fee, with funds contributing to end-of-year payouts. With an average turnout of 10 to 40 golfers, our league occasionally competes with other courses, both at home and away.

Additionally, we host the prestigious Brickshire Cup in early fall. This year, we introduce Major tournaments alongside our regular Saturday games, all offering points towards qualifying for the "Shire Cup" championship. The Shire Cup Series comprises six major events, each marked with an (SC), featuring a $10 buy-in and expanded payouts. Bonus points are available for participation in both Shire Cup and regular Saturday events, offering opportunities to secure a spot in the season-ending Top 20. We are always welcome to new members. As a member you are not required to play a certain number of times. Once your in just feel free to come whenever you want. If interested please contact us. When Do We Meet?

Tee times usually begin for the group around 8:00 am. They are usually in shotgun fashion to keep the pace of play. Most members arrive around 7:00 am at Brickshire Golf Course to hit the range and prepare for that day's round.
