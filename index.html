Brickshire Golf Leagues

About Us

We are a great group of guys and gals that love the game of golf. Each week a different game is played. These games can consist of straight score, partner best ball, 4 man scramble, and many more. You must have a handicap before joining the leauge. This makes it fair for all expeirence levels of golf. Each week a player will put $7 dollars into the pot with the potential to win a lot more. We average anywhere between 10 and 40 golfers each week. The leauge will occasionally play other course leagues, home and away. Towards the begging of fall we hold our annual Brickshire Cup! We are always welcome to new members. If intrested please contact us.
